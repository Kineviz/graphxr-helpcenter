= Manage LLMs

The *_LLM_* panel lets you add and review Large Language Models (LLMs) that can be connected to GraphXR. You can:

* Add an LLM.
* Review and update version, usage limits and other LLM details.  

*To add an LLM:*

. Open the *_LLM_* panel and click the *_Add_* button at the upper right corner.

image::/v3/10_01_LLMPanelAdd.png[,720,340,role=text-left]

. In the *_Add_* dialog, enter the required details for the LLM, including:
+
* *_Provider_*: LLM provider (i.e. OpenAI, Claude, etc.)
* *_Name_*: The name of your LLM instance (e.g.my_openai). 
* *_API URL_*:
* *_API Key_*:
* *_Organization_*:
* *_Model Name_*:
* *_Embedding Model_*:
* *_Chat Limit_*: Set maximum chat requests allowed for non-admin users per 2 hours. Zero (0) means no limit
* *_Default Model_* checkbox: Click to use the default LLM model.
+
. Click *_Save_* or *_Cancel_*.
+
When saved, the  LLM appears in the list. 
+

+
image::/v3/10_02_LLMAdded.png[,720,340,role=text-left]
+

*To update an LLM connection:*

. Open the *_LLM_* panel and click the *_Edit_* button for a connected LLM.
+
image::/v3/10_03_LLMEdit.png[,720,340,role=text-left]
+

. Enter updated details, such as *_Model Name_* and *_Chat Limit_*.
. Click *_Save_* or *_Cancel_*.

*To delete an LLM connection:*

. Open the *_LLM_* panel and click the *_Delete_* (trash can) button for the connected LLM.
+
image::/v3/10_04_LLMDelete.png[,720,340,role=text-left]
+

. Click *_Delete_* (or *_Cancel_*) in the confirmation message.
