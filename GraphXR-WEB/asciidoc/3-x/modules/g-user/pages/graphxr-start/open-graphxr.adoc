= Log In and Out of *Kineviz*

To use *Kineviz*, you'll need to create a user account, and then log in.

NOTE: See xref:desktop:desktop-login.adoc[*Install Kineviz Desktop*] to download, install, and log in to a stand-alone application.  

== Create a *Kineviz* User Account

* Go to https://graphxr.kineviz.com/login[*Kineviz* Login] to create an account.

+
NOTE: For *Kineviz _Enterprise_*, contact your system administrator for login credentials.


*To create a user account:*

. In your _Chrome_ browser, visit the https://graphxr.kineviz.com/login[*Kineviz* Login] page and click *_SIGN UP_*.
+
image::/v3/01_02_01_LoginWBrowser420.png[role=text-left]
+

. In the sign-up form, enter the information required to create your user account.
+
image::/v3/01_02_02_SignUp320.png[signin,240,420,role=text-left]
+
• Your *_Email_*. You'll use this to log in. +
• A *_Password_*. +
• The password again, in *_Confirm Password_*. +
• Your *_First Name_* and *_Last Name_*. +
• Your *_City_*. +
• Click the *_Terms of Use_* checkbox.

. Click *_Sign Up_*.
+
A message indicates that your account was successfully created (or flags errors you must fix).

== Log In to Kineviz

Once registered as a user, you can log in.

*To log in to Kineviz:*

. In your _Chrome_ browser, visit the https://graphxr.kineviz.com/login[GraphXR Login] page.
. Enter your user name (i.e. the *_Email_* used to create the account) and your *_Password_* and click *_LOGIN_*.
+
image::/v3/01_02_03_Login320.png[signin,240,320,role=text-left]
+
Upon successful login, the Kineviz *_Projects_* page displays.
+
image::/v3/01_02_04_Login_ProjectsHome.png[,720,540,role=text-left]

== Manage Your User Account

Go to the user menu at the top of the *_Projects_* page to edit your *Kineviz* account settings or log out. 

image::/v3/01_03_02_ProjectsUserMenu.png[,720,240,role=text-left]

=== Update Account Details

* Select *_Settings_* to review your account details, change your password, or delete your account. Click *_Update_* to update your account, or click *_Projects_* at the upper left of the browser to close the dialog without making any changes.

+
image::/v3/01_03_03_ProjectsUserSettings.png[,240,320,role=text-left]  
+

* Select *_Logout_* to log out of *Kineviz*.

+
NOTE: If you are an administrator of a multi-user account, you'll see an *_Admin_* item on the User menu to open your administrator panel.

===  Select UI Language 
  
Use the language selector at the top right of the *_Projects_* page to switch between default *English*, *Chinese*, or *Spanish* user interfaces.

image::/v3/01_03_04_ProjectsSpanishMenu.png[,520,140,role=text-left]

=== Access Kineviz Documentation

Click links at the bottom of the browser window to open user guides and release notes available on the Kineviz Help Center.

* *_Help_* opens *Kineviz* user documentation.
* The version number at the bottom of the browser window opens the release notes for the current *Kineviz* version.

+
image::/v3/01_03_12_VersionHelpCenterLink.png[,720,240,role=text-left]

=== User Feedback and Community 

Click links at the bottom of the browser window for:

* A *_Feedback_* form to submit questions or comments for Kineviz response.
* A *_Discord_* invitation to join the Kineviz Discord community.

== Log Out of Kineviz

*To log out of Kineviz:*

* In the *_Projects_* page, select *_Log Out_* from the user menu on the top right.

+
*OR*
+

* In an open project, click the *Kineviz logo* at the bottom left of the window, and choose *_Logout_* on the menu.
